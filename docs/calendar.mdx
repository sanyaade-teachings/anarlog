---
title: "Connect a calendar"
description: "Bring upcoming meetings into Anarlog and start them from one place."
---

Open **Calendar** in the sidebar, add an account, and enable the calendars you want Anarlog to show. Connected events appear in the calendar and timeline.

Anarlog uses calendar events for titles, times, participants, reminders, and meeting links. It does not add a bot to your calls.

## Apple Calendar

Apple Calendar is available on macOS.

1. Choose **Apple Calendar**.
2. Allow Calendar access when macOS asks.
3. Enable the calendars you want to see.

If access was denied, open **Settings → Permissions** in Anarlog. Use **Open** beside Calendar to change the macOS permission.

## Google Calendar

1. Sign in to Anarlog.
2. Choose **Google Calendar**, then finish authorization in your browser.
3. Return to Anarlog and enable the calendars you want to sync.

## Outlook Calendar

1. Sign in to Anarlog.
2. Choose **Outlook Calendar**, then finish authorization in your browser.
3. Return to Anarlog and enable the calendars you want to sync.

<Note>
  Google and Outlook connections are included with Anarlog Pro. Apple Calendar uses the calendar accounts already connected to your Mac.
</Note>

If events are missing, refresh the calendar list, confirm the calendar is enabled, and reconnect any account marked **Reconnect required**.
