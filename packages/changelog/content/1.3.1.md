## Summary customization

- Customize the complete Auto summary prompt from **Settings → Templates**, with built-in variables for the current date and language and an option to restore Anarlog's default
- Skip automatic summaries for conversations that are too short to summarize, and keep summaries proportional to shorter transcripts

## Sharing and account access

- Share from every note view, including notes created before the latest workspace updates
- Restored sign-in for accounts connected to Stripe billing

## Reliability and polish

- Download your CloudSync recovery key as a text file from a clearer, more compact setup dialog
- Switch AI providers and models without stale connection warnings flashing during the change
- See the remaining undo window in the progress bar when deleting notes
- Keep audio upload and re-transcription as separate actions when a note has no recording
